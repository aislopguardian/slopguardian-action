# Code of Conduct

## Short version

Be respectful. No harassment, no discrimination, no spam. Focus on the work.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces: issues, PRs, discussions, and any communication related to SlopGuardian.

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior can be reported to **conduct@slopguardian.dev**. Maintainers will review and take action as needed, up to and including bans.
